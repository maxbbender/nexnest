Frequently Asked Questions

What is nexnest.com?
Nexnest.com is a platform that allows college students to find rentals online through our quality internet listing service.
Is it free?
Yes, it is 100% free to search and use our services for renters! We charge landlords and property managers a flat fee to post per rental unit.
What if I don’t see any listings in my search?
If your search doesn’t return any results, it is likely that your search criteria may be too specific. We recommend starting a search with location and number of bedroom, then narrowing your search filters from there.
If we don’t currently have any properties in your area, please sign up for our newsletter to be notified when there are new listings available near you!
I am a landlord / property manager. How can I list my property?
You can Create a Listing here. Simply go through the easy step by step process of creating your listing and add it to your cart. When you’re all done, check out and your listings will be live!

Why choose nexnest?
Nexnest specializes in off-campus college rentals for both rural and urban schools. Our team of customer service professionals are dedicated to ensuring that all listings are legitimate and personally visited and vetted by nexnest.com. Our full service platform includes creating groups, scheduling tours, processing online payments, issuing maintenance requests and our messenger feature.
How do I sign up?
You can sign up a renter or a landlord here.
How do I create a group?
Once you have created an account head into My Groups which can be found in the top right menu bar. Click Create New Group. Select View Group and from there you can invite your friends.
How do I schedule a tour?
You can schedule a tour by clicking on “Schedule Tour” on any listing page. Select a time from the available timeframes shown and submit your tour request. You will be notified when the tour has been scheduled.
How do I apply for a rental?
You can apply for a rental by first, creating an account. Next, {% if current_user %} {% if not current_user.isLandlord %} create a group {% else %} create a group {% endif %} {% endif %} and invite your desired roommates to join! After that, simply click “Request this Listing” on a listing page and wait for the property owner to review your request. To see your pending and accepted requests, go to that groups page and click on "Listing Requests".
How do I sign my lease?
We currently do not handle lease signing. Once your request to lease a property listing has been accepted, please contact your landlord through the messenger to find out how to sign your lease.
How do I pay my security deposit?
We currently do not handle security deposit payments. Once your request to lease a property listing has been accepted, please contact your landlord through the messenger to find out how to pay your deposit.
How do I pay my rent?
Currently, all rent payments are not handled through nexnest.com. Please contact your landlord to obtain information on how to pay your rent.
When is my rent due?
Rent due dates are subject to the landlord’s lease agreement. Visit your house page and click "Pay Rent" for details on payment due dates.
